Rifle Club" is a 2024 Malayalam action-comedy film directed by Aashiq Abu. The story is set in 1991 and revolves around a historic rifle club in the Western Ghats. The club becomes the center of a thrilling fight for survival when a dangerous arms dealer and his gang come seeking revenge.
